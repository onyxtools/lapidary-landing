features

everything your collection needs

◈
detailed entries
Name, type, subtype, count, description, photos, tags, and categories. Everything you need to remember each stone.
⊞
grid & list views
Browse your collection as a visual gallery or a compact list. Switch instantly depending on what you need.
🔍
search & filter
Filter by type, category, or tag. Search by name or description. Find any stone in seconds.
◎
gem bundles
Group stones into named bundles — sleep pouches, altar sets, travel kits. Photos pull automatically from the member stones.
📷
photo gallery
Up to 3 photos per stone. Reorder them, view full screen, and see multi-photo thumbnails right on the card.
📝
collection notes
A personal notes space for your collection. Supports markdown — headings, lists, bold, quotes, and more.
📖
history log
Every stone added or removed is logged automatically. A record of your collection over time.
☁
cloud sync
Your collection lives in the cloud. Sign in from any device — phone, tablet, desktop — and everything is there.
✦
completely free
No subscription, no premium tier. Lapidary is free to use. If you love it, you can support the project.
